What is Queen Dagmar

Queen Dagmar is a Danish craft brewery dedicated to creating premium beers that showcase the finest locally sourced ingredients and traditional brewing techniques. Founded by Mark and Sten, the brewery places a strong emphasis on supporting local farmers and artisans from the Southern Jutland region. Their approach blends a deep respect for Danish brewing heritage with a modern twist, resulting in beers that capture the essence of the local landscape and seasons. At Queen Dagmar, each beer is thoughtfully crafted to evoke a specific time of year, making use of ingredients that reflect the surrounding environment. From the carefully selected barley malt grown on nearby farms to the fresh, locally harvested fruits, herbs, and honey, the brewery’s creations are a true celebration of Danish nature and community.

Day Maiden Ale

The Day Maiden features a refreshing alcohol content of 5.2%, meticulously crafted with locally sourced ingredients. The barley malt hails from farms around Southern Jutland, opal hops are chosen to enhance the brew's character. The original Danish ale yeast adds a touch of authenticity, and the locally sourced honey from Ribe provides a subtle sweetness, complemented by the delicate elderflower from our friends at Jensen Hildegård.

Queen Dagmar Ale

is a luxurious brew with a refined alcohol content of 8.5%. Sourced from farms around Southern Jutland, the rich barley malt serves as a robust foundation, while ruby hops are expertly selected to elevate the brew's character. The addition of original Danish ale yeast brings an authentic touch, ensuring a true representation of local craftsmanship. Infused with vibrant blackberries from local farmers in the Esbjerg municipality, this ale not only supports community businesses but also delivers a burst of fresh, fruity flavour.
